# Business Model Canvas Template

## Value Proposition

- What problem are we solving?
- Who is the target customer?
- What value do we provide?

## Customer Segments

- Who are the primary customers?
- What customer groups matter most?
- What are their key needs?

## Channels

- How do we reach customers?
- Which channels are most effective?
- What partnerships or platforms matter?

## Customer Relationships

- How do we acquire and retain customers?
- What support or engagement model do we use?
- What feedback loops exist?

## Revenue Streams

- How does the business make money?
- What are the main pricing models?
- Which revenue streams are most important?

## Key Resources

- What assets are essential?
- What technology, people, or infrastructure are required?
- What makes the offering possible?

## Key Activities

- What are the most important tasks to deliver value?
- What processes or operations matter most?
- What must be done consistently?

## Key Partnerships

- Who are our strategic partners?
- What external suppliers or collaborators matter?
- What alliances help us scale?

## Cost Structure

- What are the major costs?
- Which activities or resources are most expensive?
- What cost drivers should we monitor?
