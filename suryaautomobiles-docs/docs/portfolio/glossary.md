# Glossary

- Auto-rickshaw: Three-wheeled motorised passenger vehicle (Bajaj RE common).
- Retrofit: The act of converting ICE or legacy systems to electric drive.
- Reserve battery: The onboard solar-charged battery that supplements the main drive pack.
- Solar range extender: The combined solar panel + reserve battery + control system.
- MPPT: Maximum Power Point Tracker — optimizes solar panel energy harvesting.
- BMS: Battery Management System — manages cell balancing, SOC, safety.
