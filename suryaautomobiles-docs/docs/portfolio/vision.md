# Vision

## Purpose

SuryaExtend is a solar-first product family providing a bolt-on solar reserve module and associated retrofit kits for electric auto-rickshaws. The purpose is to reduce range anxiety for drivers, lower daily operating costs, and enable longer earning hours by adding free solar-derived range to retrofitted vehicles.

## Problem statement

- Pain: Daily range anxiety and frequent battery swaps reduce driver income and increase downtime.
- Users: Independent auto-rickshaw drivers and small fleet operators in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.
- Current state: Existing retrofits lack an integrated, low-cost solar reserve that can reliably add tens of kilometers per day.

## Proposed solution

- Hardware: A 150–180W semi-flexible rooftop solar panel, MPPT charger, 1 kWh LFP reserve battery, dual-battery BMS, and a controlled transfer relay.
- Service: Installation kits and partner workshop network for retrofit installations and field support.
- USP: Bolt-on module that provides ~40–58 km/day additional range (typical: ~48 km/day with a 150W panel) and increases driver income by reducing swapping and charging time.

Expected benefits:
- Drivers: ₹2,000–3,500/month uplift and fewer swap events.
- Fleets: Improved vehicle uptime and predictable range extension.
- Partners: New hardware and service revenue streams.

## Success criteria

- Deliver average daily solar range >= 40 km in pilot vehicles.
- Pilot adoption: 20–50 vehicles in Hyderabad within 6–12 months.
- Reliability: Transfer logic and BMS with >99% operational reliability during pilot.
- Commercial: Viable BOM and installation flow at target ASP (~₹25k) with a path to scale.
