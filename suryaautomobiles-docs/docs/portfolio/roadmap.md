# Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Validation (M0–M6)

- Confirm concept assumptions: solar yield, installation feasibility, and driver value.
- Desktop and small-scale lab tests of MPPT + BMS + transfer logic.
- Stakeholder outreach: RACE Energy, local retrofit workshops, and pilot fleets.

## Phase 1 — Prototype (M6–M12)

- Build proof-of-concept hardware and complete lab validation.
- Validate solar charging, battery transfer, and safety assumptions.
- Finalize BOM estimates and supplier shortlist.

## Phase 2 — Pilot (M12–M24)

- Run 20–50 vehicles in Hyderabad and Vijayawada.
- Collect field data: daily solar yield, driver feedback, reliability metrics.
- Iterate hardware and installation process.

## Phase 3 — Scale (M24+)

- Prepare manufacturing, QC, and partner workshops for repeatable installs.
- Expand to additional cities and fleet customers.
- Commercialize service offerings and streamline certification.
