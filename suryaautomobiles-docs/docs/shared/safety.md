# Safety

## Safety principles

- Fail-safe behavior: automatic disconnect on fault.
- Thermal management: ensure cells operate within safe limits.
- Isolation: galvanic separation from high-voltage systems where applicable.
- Human factors: clear driver instructions for transfer and maintenance.

## Hazards and mitigations

- Overcurrent: fuse and pre-charge circuits.
- Thermal runaway: cell-level monitoring and vent paths.
- Water ingress: weather-rated enclosures and connectors.
