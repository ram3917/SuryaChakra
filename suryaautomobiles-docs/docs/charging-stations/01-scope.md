# 01. Scope

Status: Draft

Visibility: Hidden from the main navigation until the infrastructure concept is approved.

## Context

The charging-stations initiative targets fleet operators and public operators who manage swap stations and shared chargers for retrofitted auto-rickshaws. It complements the range-extender product by ensuring predictable swap/charge infrastructure and integration points.

## In scope

- Integration guidance for swap stations with SuryaExtend-equipped vehicles.
- Operator workflows for accepting vehicles with dual-battery systems.
- Safety and signage requirements at charging/swapping points.

## Out of scope

- Full design of high-power public fast chargers (these are third-party).
- Grid-level energy management beyond operator site recommendations.
