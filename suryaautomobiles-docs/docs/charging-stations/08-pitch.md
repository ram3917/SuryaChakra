# 08. Pitch

## Elevator pitch

A bolt-on solar reserve module for retrofitted auto-rickshaws that adds ~48 km/day on average, reducing swap events and increasing driver earnings by ₹2,000–3,500/month.

## Value narrative

SuryaExtend converts Hyderabad's abundant daytime solar resource into direct income for drivers by harvesting rooftop solar into a reserve battery that supplements the main drive pack. The product reduces downtime, lowers running costs, and creates a repeatable retrofit revenue stream.

## Key proof points

- Prototype yield estimates and recommended panel options.
- Pilot plan to measure real-world yield and reliability.
- Clear supplier shortlist and BOM cost target.
